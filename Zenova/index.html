Faq

How does Zenova Connect work?
Zenova Connect provides a platform where consumers can compare medication prices, book appointments with verified healthcare providers, and access transparent pricing for healthcare services. For healthcare providers, we offer procurement solutions and access to verified suppliers.
Where do you currently operate?
We currently operate in Nigeria and Kenya, with plans to expand to other African markets.
Do you offer custom solutions for healthcare facilities?
Yes, we understand that each healthcare facility has unique needs. Our team can customize Zenova Connect to match your specific requirements, from specialized inventory management to custom reporting systems. Contact our solutions team to discuss your needs.
